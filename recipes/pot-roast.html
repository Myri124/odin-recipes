Easy Pot Roast

This pot roast is good.

It will feed a family of 5, and it only has 4 ingredients.

Ingredients

You need a crock pot

  • Chuck roast 2.5lbs
  • Small bag of chipped carrots or baby carrots
  • Little potatoes
  • Big can of cream of mushroom soup

Steps

  1. Put the meat in the crock pot
  2. Put the carrots in the crock pot
  3. Cut the little potatoes in half
  4. Put the little potatoes in the crock pot
  5. Dump the cream of mushroom soup on top of everything inside the crock pot
  6. Set crock pot on low

In 8 hours your pot roast will be ready to enjoy
